Introducing the HOF TORO-5006 Executive High-Back Office Desk Chair—where style meets ergonomic excellence.

The high back design offers exceptional support, encouraging healthy posture and reducing fatigue for long hours.

With a synchro single position mechanism, easily adjust the tilt to your ideal angle for a personalized experience.

TORO-5006 is engineered for durability, perfect for daily office demands. Sturdy fixed armrests provide reliable support, enhancing your comfort throughout the workday.

The powder-coated base adds a contemporary touch while ensuring stability.Glide smoothly on 50mm casters for effortless mobility across your workspace!
